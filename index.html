💡 Why does this keep appearing? Dismissing a cookie notice normally sets a cookie or uses localStorage — so the site remembers you've seen it. But this site uses neither. The reload counter in the notice (?n=… in the URL) is the only "memory" here — and it resets the moment you share the link or open a fresh tab.
This site sets exactly 0 cookies. Ironically, that's the whole problem.
